Here are some ideas about using these videos as the basis for instigating community organizing efforts and political action that will ensure that the protections guaranteed by the Flores Settlement are upheld now and in the future. The more that the experiences of people who have been held in border detention facilities are present in our local communities - our schools, our religious centers, our homes, our town centers, our arts venues - the more power this movement will have to influence the direction of national policies and legislation.
