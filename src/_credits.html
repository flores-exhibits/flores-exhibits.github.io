This project is co-created by Waterwell and the Broadway Advocacy Coalition in collaboration with the Immigrants' Rights Clinic and the Center for Institutional and Social Change at Columbia Law School. By bringing two arts-based organizations with deep roots in civic engagement into a collaboration with the rigor, policy knowledge, and research prowess of two legal and public policy organizations, The Flores Exhibits aims to affect and influence systemic change through narrative-driven advocacy.

This project is generously supported by Jayne Baron Sherman and Deby Zum through The Sherzum Fund at Our Fund. EOTI Productions, Waterwell Films, Butter Studio and Sam Sweeney contributed in-kind services.
