Exhibits

In June 2019, a team of lawyers went to detention facilities at the U.S. / Mexico border, and discovered the conditions there did not meet the requirements laid out in the Flores Settlement.

This team of lawyers took 65 sworn testimonies from children and young adults – often young parents – detailing their experience in the detention facilities.

These 65 sworn testimonies were submitted as exhibits attached to a motion for a temporary restraining order filed by the National Center for Youth Law on June 26, 2019 which seeks to provide immediate relief to children living without access to basic hygiene supplies and adequate nutrition or sleep.
